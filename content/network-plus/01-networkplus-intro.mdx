# 📡 CompTIA Network+ (N10-009) - Introduction

Welcome to the **CompTIA Network+** documentation! This guide is designed to help you understand, prepare, and succeed in earning your **Network+ certification** — a globally recognized credential for network professionals.

---

## 🧠 What is CompTIA Network+?

**CompTIA Network+** validates your skills in:

- 🧩 **Networking Fundamentals**
- 🌐 **Network Implementations** (wired, wireless, and cloud)
- 🛠️ **Troubleshooting Techniques**
- 🔒 **Security Concepts**
- 🗂️ **Network Operations & Management**

It's **vendor-neutral**, meaning it's applicable to all platforms and environments — a great stepping stone for networking and cybersecurity careers.

---

## 📝 Exam Snapshot

Here’s what you need to know before you register:

- **🧪 Exam Code:** N10-009 (latest version)  
- **🕒 Duration:** 90 minutes  
- **❓ Questions:** Up to 90 (multiple choice + performance-based)  
- **📈 Passing Score:** 720 / 900  
- **🌍 Available Languages:** English, Japanese, and more  
- **🏷️ Cost:** ~$358 USD (may vary by region)

---

## 🚀 Why Should You Take It?

- ✅ Builds a solid **foundation in networking**
- ✅ Opens up opportunities in **IT support, helpdesk, network admin, and security**
- ✅ Prepares you for advanced certifications like **Security+**

## 📅 How to Take the Exam?

You have **two ways** to take the Network+ exam:

### 💻 Online (Remote Proctored)

- Take it from home via **Pearson VUE**
- Requires webcam, stable internet, and a **quiet space**
- ID verification and room scan are mandatory

### 🏢 In-Person (Test Center)

- Book a seat at a **Pearson VUE test center**
- Secure and distraction-free testing environment
